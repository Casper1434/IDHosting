---
title: 'How to reset VPS password?'
description: 'Learn how to reset password'
---


<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1.">
    Go to VPS Management Page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2.">
   Click on "Change Password"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3.">
    Enter New Password and then click on "Change Password"
  </Step>
    <Step title="Step 4.">
    Stop/Start your VPS.
  </Step>
    <Step title="Step 5.">
    Your Root Password will be changed successfully!
  </Step>
</Steps>
